Play Your Cards Right, Higher or Lower?

Target:

The aim of the game is to guess whether the next card will be higher or lower than the current card. If you guess all 6 cards correctly you complete the game. If you guess incorrectly you lose and have to start over.

Game Layout:

  1. 6 Cards are displayed onscreen
  2. First Card number is displayed, remaining cards are hidden
  3. Card numbers can range from 0-15

How To Play:

  1. Step One
    • The First Card is displayed (eg. 1). The next card is hidden
  2. Go ahead and make a guess
    3. There are two buttons you can choose from "Higher" or "Lower"
  3. You must now guess if the next card will be "Higher" or "Lower" based on your current card number
  4. Next Card Revealed:
    • After your guess is submitted, the next card is revealed
  5. One of two things will happen:
    6. If you guess correctly:
    • The Card will turn green and you move onto the next round
    • A New card is presentated, repeat the guessing process
  6. If you guess incorrectly:
    • Card turns red and the game ends
    • If you want to play again new cards will be presented and you start from the first card

Complete the game:

If you correctly guess all 6 cards, you have completed the game

Losing the Game:

If you guess incorrectly at any point, you will have the option to reset the game and start again
